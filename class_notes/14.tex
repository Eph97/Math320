\subsection{$L^{p}$-spaces}

Recall definition of $L^{1}$.

$m$ is a measure on $\sigma$-algebra $S$ on a set $X$.

$\mathcal{L}^{1}(m)$ is the space of measurable $f : [-\infty, \infty]$ such that

\begin{align*}
	[f]_{\mathcal{L}^{1}(m)} = \int |f| dm < \infty
\end{align*}
